Motivation

Why doesn't minimising training error work?

📉
We fit a model
Minimise error on the training set.
The model adjusts all parameters
to match observed data as closely
as possible.
🎯
We want generalisation
The real goal is low error on new,
unseen data — drawn from the same
process that generated the
training set.
💡
The gap
A model can achieve zero training error and still fail badly on new data.
