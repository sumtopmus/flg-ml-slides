§ 4 — Estimation & Practice

Diagnosing your model — which term dominates?

Bias² Dominates (underfitting)
Symptoms
  • • Training error is high
  • • Test error similarly high — small gap
  • • Learning curves plateau early
Variance Dominates (overfitting)
Symptoms
  • • Training error very low
  • • Test error much higher — large gap
  • • Performance varies across splits
  • • More data helps
