§ 6 — Checklist

Operational questions for any ML system

1
What assumptions create systematic error? Bias
Model family, feature engineering, label definition
2
What parts of the pipeline are unstable? Variance
Random seeds, split sensitivity, prompt wording, batch effects
3
Which subgroups are underrepresented? Data
Demographic, geographic, temporal, edge cases
4
How does performance change under distribution shift? Shift
Resampling, prompt changes, domain/time shift, adversarial inputs
5
Are confidence estimates calibrated? Calibration
ECE, reliability diagrams, temperature scaling, proper scoring rules
