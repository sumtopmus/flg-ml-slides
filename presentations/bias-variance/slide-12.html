§ 3 — MSE Theorem

MSE as a bias-variance decomposition. General case

MSE  =  Variance  +  Bias²  +  σ²
𝔼D[(f̂ − Y)²]  =  𝔼D[(f̂ − 𝔼[f̂])²]  +  (𝔼[f̂] − f)²  +  Var(ε|X)
# σ² = randomness in the data-generating process itself, not removable by choosing a better estimator of f(x) alone
# σ²(x) is used instead of σ² if the noise level depends on x
σ² is usually omitted because it's assumed that σ² is the same for all models e.g. errors in target or unobserved features which influence the target
