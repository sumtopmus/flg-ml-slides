Layer Norm Choices
01
RMS Norm
Instead of full Layer Norm, use RMSNorm — normalize by root mean square only.

Simpler, faster, and empirically matches LayerNorm quality.
02
Pre-Norm
Apply norm before the sublayer (attention / FFN), not after.

More stable gradient flow during training. Dominant in modern LLMs.
03
No Bias in FFN
Drop the bias term in the final linear layer before softmax.

Reduces parameters with minimal quality loss.
Post-Norm (old)
x → Sublayer → Norm(x + residual)
Pre-Norm (modern)
x → Norm(x) → Sublayer → + residual
