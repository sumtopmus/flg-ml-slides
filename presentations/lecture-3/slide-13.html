G groups of Q heads share one K, V. Between MHA and MQA. Used in LLaMA 2/3, Mistral.
Why it matters: The KV cache is the main memory bottleneck during inference. MQA/GQA reduce cache size proportionally to the number of KV heads, enabling longer contexts and larger batches on the same GPU.
